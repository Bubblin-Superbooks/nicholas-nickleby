Chapter 59

The Plots Begin To Fail, And Doubts And Dangers To Disturb The Plotter_

Ralph sat alone, in the solitary room where he was accustomed to take his meals, and to sit of nights when no profitable occupation called him abroad. Before him was an untasted breakfast, and near to where his fingers beat restlessly upon the table, lay his watch.
